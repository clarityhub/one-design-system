# Forms

Forms are a standard way for users to provide, submit,
and edit data.

* Use labelled form fields to provide accessibile ways
for users to enter data.
* Use input groups to space out fields correctly.

## Examples

### Simple (Uncontrolled) Form

<Playground>
    <Typography>Tell us about yourself:</Typography>
    <InputGroup>
        <LabelledInput label="First Name" />
    </InputGroup>
    <InputGroup>
        <LabelledInput label="Last Name" />
    </InputGroup>
    <InputGroup>
        <LabelledTextarea label="Short bio" />
    </InputGroup>
    <InputGroup>
        <Typography type="sectionLabel">What gender do you identify with?</Typography>

            <LabelledRadio value="noselection" label="Choose not to disclose" />
        </RadioGroup>
    </InputGroup>
</Playground>
